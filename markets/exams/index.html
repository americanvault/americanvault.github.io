Certification

Markets credentials, earned and held to standard

Anyone can finish a course. A Demetri Code credential certifies something harder: that the holder can read the doctrine, plan to standard, and execute with discipline under real conditions. The Trading Doctrine uses written examinations and demonstrated competencies, not attendance.

The Markets ladder

The course credentials, Certified TDC Foundations, Analyst, and Practitioner, are each earned by passing that level's written examination, and a self-study reader can sit them. Above them sits the professional capstone, TDC-Certified: earned only after a certified instructor signs off every demonstrated competency, then a proctored examination across four instruments.

A credential is earned, never bought. Examination standards and renewal terms publish with the materials.
